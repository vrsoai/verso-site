How it works

A model's confidence tells you nothing about whether it's right.

A second answer does. Verso sits in the margin of whatever assistant you already use and gives you the other side of the page.

STEP 01

Install once

Verso opens as a panel next to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity. Nothing to configure.

STEP 02

Ask normally

Keep working the way you always do. When an answer matters, Verso taps you on the shoulder.

STEP 03

Read the verso

Your question goes to a second model. Two answers, side by side. You decide which one earned it.
